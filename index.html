God's number is 20, mine is 67.

The term "God's number" is the minimum number of turns required to solve a Rubik's cube from an arbitrary starting position (i.e., in the worst case). Rokicki et al. (2010) used a Google supercomputer and 35 CPU-years to determine God's number to be 20. Without using magic or trickery, the best I can do is 67. Learn one of the most common algorithms to solve the Rubik's cube.
